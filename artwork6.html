Chaotic Places at SJSU

Digital Video, 2020

This project was created using Adobe Premiere Pro. For my Digital Video Post Project, I originally wanted to do it on the comparison between chaotic places vs. peaceful places at San Jose State University. I ended up switching it up a little bit and decided to just do it in chaotic places around SJSU. This project could be shown pretty much anywhere. My influence for this reflects how I see San Jose State University after attending here for almost two years.
