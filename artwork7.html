Lets Find Out What Movie Fits You

Interactive Net Art, 2018

This interactive net art piece was created using Atom and Github. The whole concept of my Net Art project was to be like a survey. I wanted my audience to take different paths so that people would have unique results of what movie that is chosen for them because everyone has different interests and I thought it would be cool to kinda showcase that on my website.
