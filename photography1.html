Light

Photography, 2020

For this project, we focused on the theme of light, its characteristics, and feeling. It was essential to focus on the use of light creatively and to create compositions where lighting enhances the subject or is used to bring attention to a particular part of my subject. I ended up focusing on the sun and how it lights up our environments like my park and my house. Photos were shot on a Nikon D60 and edited in Adobe Lightroom.
