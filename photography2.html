Daily Life in Quarantine

Photography, 2020

For this project, we again focused on the theme of light. In addition, we had to think about what makes someone or yourself who they are as well as how to capture the idea on camera while also focusing on the forms of light. I wanted to show my viewers what my daily life has been like. All of the photos were shot on a Nikon D60 and edited in Adobe Lightroom.
