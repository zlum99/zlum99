Mandala

Digital Image, 2017

This art piece was created using Adobe Photoshop. I was inspired to create my own mandala after seeing other artistsâ€™ works that are similar. The process was fairly simple and all I had to do to make the mandala was make multiple copies (around 3 or 4) of one image and rotate them. After creating the mandala shape I used different blending modes to make the multiple images bleed into each other to make the change seamlessly. The final touches were creating backgrounds for the mandala in order for it to stick out more.
