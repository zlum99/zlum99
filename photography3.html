This Land

Photography, 2020

For this project, I explored the theme of "This Land" and what it means to me through the lens of landscape photography. I decided to take pictures of my hometown where I currently live. I went to different locations around my hometown city and took pictures of areas that represent my city. All of the photos were shot on a Nikon D60 and edited in Adobe Lightroom.
