Human and Nature Boundaries

Photography, 2020

My photographs in this piece are supposed to represent the boundaries between nature and human life. In this case, this project was inspired by some of the photos I took from the “This Land” assignment. I decided I wanted to focus entirely on where we can see these boundaries between humans and nature. I went to different locations around my hometown where I thought I could see this representation. The difference between this project from my previous projects is that I decided I wanted to work in color with my photos because I felt that the images will showcase my concept stronger rather than it being black and white. All of the photos were shot on a Nikon D60 and edited in Adobe Lightroom.
