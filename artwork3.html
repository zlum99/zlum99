The City Life

Digital Sound, 2018

The Digital Sound was created in Audacity and then uploaded into Soundcloud. The Digital Image that goes along with the sound art was created in Adobe Photoshop. This piece includes two original recording sounds and one sound from the internet. The two original sounds I recorded were cars driving down South Tenth Street and the noise from the student union. The sound I grabbed from the internet is sirens from a fire truck, ambulance, and police. With these three sounds, I wanted my audience to put on headphones and see and hear what a city environment would be like. I envision my audience to also be present in a city while listening to my audio. For the best experience, present the image with the guy with headphones looking into Times Square on the whole screen and put headphones on if possible.
